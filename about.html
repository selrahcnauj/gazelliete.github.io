Our History

One day while shopping, the group noticed the lackness of customers of the local brands compared to their foreign and
much well known brands. The group investigate and found out that, Filipinos are most likely to choose foreign brands over local brands due to their mindset that all locally made products are low quality, although it possess the same quality and materials that foreign products has. So they come up with the idea of establishing their own clothing line using the local resources, they partnered with various local manufacturer and supplier to supply high grade fabrics made from locally grown cotton and other stuffs. The group finally opened their first branch near the highway.

The once just an investigation is now inline with the most prestigious and famous Filipino brands in the country and abroad.
